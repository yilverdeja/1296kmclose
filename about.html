About 1296KmClose

1296KmClose was created throughout the 2020 Coronavirus Pandemic that engulfed the world. Borders closed, Lockdowns imposed, and People were separated for months.

1296 kilometers apart, between Laos and Hong Kong, we decided to create our own memory despite never having met in-person.
